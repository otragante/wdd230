What is the target audience?

The Today's Weather's target audience is whoever person who wants to know how the weather will be before to do some activity. These people could be a young woman who doesn't know what to do on a Saturday morning; or a senior man who wants to know where he can buy a new stylish umbrella for the coming winter.
